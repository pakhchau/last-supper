Your answer becomes one of eight courses served that evening.

✦
The Ritual
How the evening unfolds
I

The Question

Weeks before the dinner, a mysterious chef reaches out to each guest privately. One question: your last meal. Your answer is sealed. No one else will see it.

II

The Preparation

The chef transforms eight answers into an eight-course tasting menu. A Shake Shack craving becomes a deconstructed truffle burger. Grandmother's congee becomes a silken rice velouté. Nothing is literal. Everything is interpreted.

III

The Gathering

Eight guests. One long table. The menu is placed before you — eight courses, no names attached. You don't know whose soul you're about to taste.

IV

The Guessing

With each course, the table speculates. Whose last meal is this? The quiet one? The one who travels? The one who never talks about home? Arguments arise. Laughter erupts. You learn more about your friends in these moments than in years of small talk.

V

The Reveal

One by one, each guest claims their course. And explains why. The room goes quiet. Because what you'd eat before you die is never really about food — it's about memory, comfort, love, and who you were before the world told you who to be.
